    Agents

    When selling your property with the help of a real estate agent, there are two ways to go about it. The first and the most common option is called intermediation or multible listing agreement. This is where you allow multible agencies to promote your home and the agent who successfully closes the deal receives commission. The second is an exclussive representation contract, wher you grant the sole right to sell your property to one broker. Now, the question is which option is more beneficial for you?
